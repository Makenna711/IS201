About Me

This is what I do.

Hey it's me! Makenna VanOrman, allow me to introduce myself. I'm a student in the Marriott School of Business at Brigham Young University. I'm currently a sophmore working on a bachlors degree in marketing.

I grew up here in Utah, with super accessible mountains, I've developed lots of outdoor hobbies such as hiking, skiing, mountain biking, and especially rappelling. I love rappelling so much that you'll have to check out my other page about it!

One of the biggest things that has changed my life was spending a year and a half on an LDS mission speaking spanish in Toronto, Canada. I loved the message I was sharing with others so much that I continue to prepare others to do the same by working as a Spanish teacher at the Missionary Training Center. I love what I do!
